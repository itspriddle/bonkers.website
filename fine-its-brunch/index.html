Our Story

We Never Asked
For This.

We opened in 2019 as a simple neighborhood spot. Breakfast, lunch, dinner. All day. Every day. You could order pancakes at 9pm. A burger at 7am. This was always the deal.

Then the questions started. "Do you do brunch?" We'd say, "Everything is available all day." They'd nod, look confused, and ask again: "Yeah but do you do brunch?"

"Great food. Wish they had brunch :("
— An actual 1-star review from someone who ordered breakfast food. At brunch time.

After 847 emails, 312 Instagram DMs, and one very passionate letter written on a cloth napkin, we caved.

So here it is. Brunch. The same food. New names. Higher prices. Served in a 4-hour window on weekends. You're welcome.

"The space hasn't changed. We just added Edison bulbs and called it 'ambiance.'"

Dietary Accommodations

  • We are happy to accommodate all dietary needs, including ones you diagnosed yourself using a BuzzFeed quiz.
  • Yes, the steak is gluten-free. It always was. It's a steak.
  • We can make anything dairy-free. We can also add extra cheese to your dairy-free order, like last time.
  • Our oat milk comes from oats. We are told this is important.
  • If you have a legitimate allergy, we take that extremely seriously. If you "just feel better without gluten" after reading a blog post, we will nod supportively.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your brunch hours?
Saturday and Sunday, 10am–2pm. Every other hour of every other day, we serve the identical food and call it by its real name.
Do you take reservations for brunch?
Yes. You can also just walk in during any other meal and sit right down. But I understand the reservation is part of the experience.
Is brunch available on weekdays?
No. On weekdays it's called "eating breakfast" or "eating lunch." Same food. Different social construct.
Can I have a mimosa outside of brunch hours?
Yes. We have orange juice. We have champagne. We have glasses. This technology has existed for some time.
Why is the brunch menu more expensive?
Wooden boards aren't free, and apparently neither is my dignity.
Do you have bottomless mimosas?
Yes. They are bottomless because the glass has an open top, which is how all glasses work.
What's the difference between your regular menu and the brunch menu?
Fonts, mostly.
